About AR

Craftsmanship, elegance, and exclusivity

The Essence of AR

AR Perfume was born from a belief that fragrance should be more than an accessory—it should be an extension of identity. We create for those who value presence, tradition, and the quiet confidence that comes with the finest things in life.

Each bottle is a commitment to excellence. Our attars and perfumes blend time-honoured ingredients with modern refinement, resulting in scents that are at once distinctive and timeless. We do not follow trends; we define presence.

Craft & Identity

From sourcing to formulation, every step is guided by a single principle: only the exceptional is worthy of the AR name. Our collections are limited, our standards are not. We invite you to discover a fragrance that speaks to who you are—and who you choose to become.
